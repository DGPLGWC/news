Surreal Pictures Show Jupiter Is Even Weirder Than We Thought

The solar system's biggest planet just keeps getting more spectacular -and more confoundingly mysterious. While we've known for centuries that Jupiter is snazzily decorated with colorful, cloudy bands and stormy splotches, the images coming back from NASA's Juno spacecraft, which has been orbiting Jupiter since last July, reveal filigreed clouds unlike anything else in the solar system.
